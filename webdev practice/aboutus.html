About Us

//Welcome To Galacticos Network

TALENT FOR TODAY'S TASKS AND
TOMORROW'S CHALLENGES.

  • Galacticos Network was founded in 2015 with an idea to provide IT services with a high level of transparency.

  • The idea became a reality with our first project that focused on the delivery and global implementation of an ERP system. Galacticos Network reach extended to Brazil, Uganda, Zambia, Argentina, Indonesia, United Kingdom and USA within a very short span of time

  • We were appraised as a CMMI Level III company in 2019. As the company progressed, we forayed into solution development and now, our client portfolio has grown to include some of the big names in the finance, agribusiness and government sectors

  • Galacticos Network footprint extends across India, USA, UAE and Australia with offices strategically located in Hyderabad, Mumbai, Dubai and Dallas.
